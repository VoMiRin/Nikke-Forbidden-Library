@@@SUB_TITLE: A Nikke's Burden
[SCENE START]
Location: Ark - Rapi's Quarters - Night
SOUND: Soft hum of the Ark's systems, gentle rain against the window (simulated)
(RAPI is alone, looking at an old, slightly damaged data chip in her hand.)
NARRATION: Even within the metallic shell of a Nikke, memories could linger like phantom limbs.
RAPI: (To herself, softly) It's been so long...
(Flashback sequence begins - visual filter, slightly desaturated colors)
YOUNG RAPI (VOICEOVER): Unit 734, reporting for duty. My designation... is Rapi.
MENTOR NIKKE (VOICEOVER): Welcome to the line, rookie. Forget your past. Your only purpose now is to fight.
(Flashback shows a younger, less experienced RAPI in a chaotic battle, making a mistake that endangers her squad.)
MENTOR NIKKE: Rapi! What are you doing?! Stick to the protocol!
YOUNG RAPI: I... I thought I saw an opening! I'm sorry!
(End Flashback sequence. Rapi clenches her fist around the data chip.)
RAPI: Mistakes... They define us as much as our victories.
(The COMMANDER enters her quarters, having knocked lightly.)
COMMANDER: Rapi? Everything alright? You seemed troubled.
RAPI: (Quickly hides the data chip) Commander. Just... reflecting. Old memories.
COMMANDER: Want to talk about it? Sometimes it helps.
RAPI: (Hesitates, then gives a small smile) Perhaps another time. For now, there is work to be done. The past should stay where it belongs.
COMMANDER: If you say so. But know that you're not alone in carrying burdens.
RAPI: Thank you, Commander. That means... a lot.
[SCENE END]
